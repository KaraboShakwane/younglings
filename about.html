What we are all about!

The company was founded 2010 by Mr Erick Becker in the Northern Cape, which is a province in South Africa. The company went shortly in business right after the 2010 World Cup, it was during that time when the vision was birthed.
The company believes in grooming young men and validating their dreams. The company select a selected number of the hundreds of applications it recieves every year. The selected few are then taken to a camp where they are evaluated then designited mentors within their interest.These individuals are given assessments throughout to expand their knowledge within their respective interests.
